# AutoStream - Comprehensive Knowledge Base

## Welcome to AutoStream
AutoStream is a premiere AI-powered SaaS video editing suite built explicitly to empower modern content creators, YouTubers, and social media managers. It eliminates the steep learning curve of traditional NLEs (Non-Linear Editors) by leveraging generative AI to automate cuts, captions, and color grading.

---

## 1. Subscription Plans & Pricing

### Basic Plan
- **Price**: $29/month (Billed monthly) or $290/year (Save 16%)
- **Export Limit**: 10 videos per month
- **Resolution**: Up to 720p HD
- **Storage**: 50GB Cloud Workbench Storage
- **Core Features**: 
  - Basic Timeline Editor
  - Standard Transitions & Text Overlays
  - Direct Social Media Publishing (1 account)
  
### Pro Plan (Most Popular)
- **Price**: $79/month (Billed monthly) or $790/year (Save 16%)
- **Export Limit**: Unlimited videos
- **Resolution**: Up to 4K Ultra-HD
- **Storage**: 1TB Cloud Workbench Storage
- **Premium AI Features**:
  - **Auto-Captions**: AI generates 99.5% accurate subtitles in 50+ languages.
  - **Smart-Cut**: Automatically detects and trims silences and filler words (um, uh).
  - **AI Color Grading**: Cinematic one-click color correction.
  - **Audio Enhance**: Studio-quality background noise removal.
- **Bonus**: Multi-account social media integration (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels) API pushing.

## 2. Core Features & Capabilities

### Supported Formats
- **Import**: MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, WebM. (Max 50GB per raw file)
- **Export**: Handbrake optimized MP4 algorithms, ProRes (Pro Plan Only), and direct cloud syncing.

### Collaborative Workspaces (Enterprise / Teams)
Currently, AutoStream focuses on solo-creators. However, Team collaboration features (multi-editor sync) are in closed Beta and will officially release in Q4 2026.

## 3. Company Policies & Support

### Refund Policy (Strictly Enforced)
- **7-Day Guarantee**: Users can request a 100% full refund within 7 days of their initial purchase, provided they have exported fewer than 3 videos.
- **After 7 Days**: No refunds are offered. Cancellations will simply stop the next billing cycle, leaving the account active until the end of the paid month.

### Customer Support SLAs
- **Basic Plan**: Email-only ticket support. Responses typically within 48-72 business hours.
- **Pro Plan**: 24/7 Priority Live Chat Support and dedicated 1-on-1 Zoom onboarding sessions available on request.

## 4. Privacy & Data Security
All uploaded raw footages are secured via AES-256 AWS S3 encryption. Videos are strictly inaccessible to AutoStream staff and are permanently deleted 30 days after subscription termination.
